    How VaultTag Works

    Four steps from physical product to blockchain-verified digital asset

    1

    Mint NFT

    Admin creates an on-chain NFT certificate for each product with metadata stored on IPFS.

    2

    Tag Product

    An NFC sticker with the token ID is attached to the shoe. Its hardware UID is bound on-chain.

    3

    Scan & Verify

    Anyone can tap their phone to the NFC tag or enter the token ID online. Blockchain confirms authenticity.

    4

    Own & Redeem

    Verified owners can redeem their NFT — a permanent, one-time action recorded on the blockchain.
